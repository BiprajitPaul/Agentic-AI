Our Story

Founded in 2005 by Chef Antoine Dubois, The Gourmet Table was born from a passion for classic French cuisine with a modern twist. We believe in sourcing the freshest local ingredients and combining them with traditional techniques to create a truly unique dining experience.

Our commitment to excellence extends from our kitchen to our elegant dining room, where every guest is treated to impeccable service. Come and discover why we're a beloved culinary destination.
